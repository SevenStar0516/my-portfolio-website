Early Life

Hello! I'm Sean Fei, a 20-year-old born in Canada. Shortly after my birth, I moved to China to immerse myself in Chinese culture and learn the language. At age 11, my family returned to Canada, where I focused on improving my English and adapting to Canadian culture.

My Hobbies

In my free time, I enjoy video games, soccer, and photography. Gaming fascinates me, especially the design and development aspects. I actively play badminton in local clubs and participate in tournaments, which keeps me active and teaches teamwork. Photography allows me to capture and create beautiful moments, both digitally and on film.

Current State

I am a third-year student at the University of Toronto Mississauga (UTM), majoring in Communication, Culture, Information & Technology (CCIT) and Technology, Coding & Society (TCS). I have a strong interest in web design and graphic design, proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Adobe Creative Suite. Living in Canada suits me well, as I appreciate the slower-paced lifestyle that supports my academic and personal growth.
